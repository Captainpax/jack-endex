------------>=-Passives-=<-------------

Passives do not have a cost to activate, but do take up a skill slot at all times.
All Boost and Amp skills do stack.
[Elem] Refers to the fact that literally any element with an affinity could be inserted here.

The resist/block/reflect/drain passives are useful when using equipment that edits your affinities
They are also useful on demons who have been fused and carry over the skill, or level up into losing a weakness
Example: Pixie is weak to Dark, but at LV15 she could gain Resist Dark as a passive and is no longer weak to it


     NAME       | DESCRIPTION
----------------|--------------------------
Poison Boost    | Boosts the chance of poison to hit by 10%, and the damage by 100%
Poison Amp      | Boosts the chance of poison to hit by 20%, and the damage by 200%

[Elem] Boost    | Boosts the damage of [Elem] by 25%
[Elem] Amp      | Boosts the damage of [Elem] by 50%
Resist [Elem]   | Raises resistance to [Elem] to Resist. Cannot lower affinity.
Block [Elem]    | Raises resistance to [Elem] to Block. Cannot lower affinity.
Reflect [Elem]  | Raises resistance to [Elem] to Reflect. Cannot lower affinity, or change from Drain.
Drain [Elem]    | Raises resistance to [Elem] to Drain. Cannot lower affinity, or change from Reflect. 

Divine Grace    | Raises effectiveness of Healing Spells by 50%
Fountain of Life| Raises effectiveness of Healing Spells by 100%
Heat Riser      | Reduces the cost of Attack spells by 25%
Fury of Yasogami| Reduces the cost of Physical Skills by 25%
